Child Safety Standards

Dark Secrets & Confessions is committed to protecting users, especially minors, from abuse, exploitation, and harmful content.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We have a zero-tolerance policy for any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or exploitation. Any such content is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate action, including account suspension and reporting to relevant authorities.

2. User Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed promptly, and necessary action is taken.

3. Content Moderation

We actively monitor and moderate content to detect and prevent harmful or illegal activity.

4. Cooperation with Authorities

We comply with all applicable laws and cooperate with law enforcement agencies when required.
