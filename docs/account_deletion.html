Delete Account – Unveil Zim If you wish to delete your Unveil Zim account and associated data, follow these steps: 1. Open the Unveil Zim app. 2. Go to Profile Settings. 3. Select "Delete Account". 4. Confirm deletion. Once deleted: • Your profile information will be permanently removed. • Your uploaded photos will be deleted. • Your messages will be removed from your account. • Some limited data may be retained temporarily for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes. If you are unable to access your account, you may request deletion by emailing: support@unveilzim.com We process deletion requests within 7 days.
