Delete Account – Unveil Zim

If you would like to delete your Unveil Zim account and associated data, please request deletion using the method below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Email us at: support@unveilzim.com
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include the email address you used to register your Unveil Zim account.

Processing time: We process deletion requests within 7 days.

What data is deleted

  • Account profile information (e.g., name, preferences, profile settings)
  • Uploaded profile photos (if applicable)
  • Content associated with your account (e.g., posts/comments linked to your profile, where applicable)

Data retention

Some limited data may be retained temporarily for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes. If any retention applies, it will be limited to the minimum period required for those purposes.
