Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data from Dark Secrets & Confessions, please follow the steps below:

Steps to request account deletion:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to your profile/settings
  3. Select "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

If you are unable to access the app, you can request deletion by emailing us:

Email: tasararusike@gmail.com

What data is deleted:

  • Your profile information
  • Your messages and conversations
  • Your uploaded content

Data retention:

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days for security, fraud prevention, or legal compliance purposes. After this period, all data is permanently deleted.
