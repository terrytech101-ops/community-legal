Child Safety & Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

Unveil Zim is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users.

Zero Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Sexual exploitation of minors
  • Grooming behavior
  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Solicitation involving minors

Age Restrictions

Unveil Zim is strictly for users aged 18 and over.

Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content or behavior within the app. Reports related to child safety are reviewed promptly and may be reported to relevant authorities.

Enforcement

Accounts found violating child safety policies will be permanently removed.
